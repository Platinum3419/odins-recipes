Spicy Lime Grilled Shrimp

Recipe for creating Grilled Shrimp with rice.

Grilled shrimp is jazzed up with a little cajun seasoning and lime juice. Easy to create with only 4 ingredients and steps, only taking around 30 minutes to prep, cook and do whatever else you need to do to make sure they're looking good.

Ingredients

  • tablespoons Cajun seasoning (such as Emeril's Essence or any alternative seasoning)
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 pound peeled and deveined medium shrimp (30-40 per pound)

Steps

  1. Mix together Cajun seasoning (or an alternative), lime juice, and oil in a resealable plastic bag. Add shrimp, coat with marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal the bag. Marinate in the refrigerator for 20 minutes.
  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  3. Remove shrimp from marinade; shake off excess. Discard remaining marinade.
  4. Cook shrimp on the preheated grill until bright pink on the outside and the meat is no longer transparent in the center, about 2 minutes per side.
